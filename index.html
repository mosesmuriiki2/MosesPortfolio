About Me

Hello there! I'm Moses Muriiki Ngicu, hailing from the picturesque town of Meru in Kenya. As a dedicated full-stack engineer at Tangazoletu Limited, I've immersed myself in the world of financial services, shaping innovative solutions that drive mobile banking, credit scoring, and management portals. My journey in tech is powered by a passion for creating robust, scalable applications using cutting-edge technologies such as Java, Spring Boot, Kotlin, Vue.js, and React.js.

Beyond the screen, my life is a blend of diverse interests and experiences. I'm an avid movie buff, with a soft spot for cinematic storytelling that captivates and inspires. Books are my escape, offering new worlds and ideas to explore, while a cup of coffee fuels my creative process as I code through challenges. Traveling is another passion of mine—each new destination brings fresh perspectives and unforgettable experiences that enrich my personal and professional life.

In addition to my role at Tangazoletu, I'm proud to be the founder and CTO of AfyaSoftPlus (ASP), a dynamic startup with a mission to enhance mental health through technology. Our platform is designed to bridge the gap between users and mental health specialists, offering options for both physical and virtual consultations. We are also excited about integrating virtual reality into our services, aiming to provide immersive and supportive environments for our users.

Family is a cornerstone of my life—I cherish my siblings and the shared moments we create. As a dog lover, my furry friends bring joy and companionship into my everyday routine. When it comes to sports, my heart beats for Manchester United, and I never miss an opportunity to catch an NBA game, where I’m always inspired by the incredible talent and teamwork on display.

In essence, I’m driven by a relentless curiosity and a desire to make a meaningful impact. Whether it's through my professional endeavors or personal interests, I am committed to continuous growth, learning, and contributing to the tech community. Let’s connect and explore how we can create something amazing together!
